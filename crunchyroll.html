User Onboarding Teardown

Crunchyroll

Connecting anime and manga fans across 200+ countries and territories with the content and experiences they love.

Overview

  • Crunchyroll was founded in 2006 and was recently acquired by Sony for $1.175Bn, merging with major competitor Funimation.
  • It provides access to a large library of licensed anime content in multiple languages for viewers worldwide.
  • The platform offers both free ad-supported content and premium subscription content.
  • Crunchyroll serves the anime community across various verticals, including events, theatrical releases, games, consumer products, collectibles, and manga publishing.

Value Propositions

Simulcast Streaming new releases as soon as they get aired in Japan, decreasing wait time.
Dubbed and Subtitled Episodes are made available in different languages or added subtitles for worldwide users.
Offline Viewing Episodes can be downloaded to enable users to watch without an internet connection.
Bridging the Gap Helping users find and watch an extensive anime library from anywhere in the world.

Key UX Learnings

App Store Highlights

  • Prominently features trending anime to grab visitor attention.
  • Highlights the core value proposition: access to a vast library.

Trust & Onboarding

  • Login page utilizes social proof by displaying popular anime banners to foster trust.
  • Subscription tiers use playful names (Fan, Mega Fan) paired with animated characters for connection.
  • Minimalist login/signup design aligns with the app's overall theme.

Content Discovery

  • Newly added features are introduced directly on the home page to prevent navigation frustration.
  • Well-timed pop-up banners for recent additions feature direct "Watch Now" buttons.

Strategic Recommendations

Reduce Onboarding Friction

Implement social login options like Google or Facebook to allow effortless joining with existing credentials, minimizing onboarding steps.

Interactive Onboarding Guide

Add an animated character assistant to provide targeted guidance based on user actions, making the journey more entertaining and engaging.

Gamification

Introduce "Tier Badges" based on total watch time to add challenge, foster a sense of accomplishment, and encourage users to explore the library.
