System Architecture Blueprint

Universal Recommendation Engine

A state-of-the-art machine learning architecture designed to unify Things, Media, and Services. Combining collaborative filtering, semantic vector search, and a dual-layer AI (Supervised + Reinforcement Learning) optimized purely for long-term product KPIs.

Reinforcement Learning Vector Search Product Analytics Knowledge Graphs

The Engineering Philosophy

"Product Analytics is not separate from the recommendation system. Product Analytics defines the KPIs, supervised learning predicts short-term actions that lead toward those KPIs, and reinforcement learning optimizes the long-term sequence of recommendations that maximizes those KPIs over time. It is a combination of analytics, prediction, and decision optimization."

Layer 8

Reinforcement Learning

While SL asks "Will the user click?", RL asks "What recommendation sequence maximizes future lifetime value?"

Policy Learning Journey
[State: Profile & History]
  ↓
[Policy Network]
  ↓

Layer 9

Re-Ranking & Business Logic

Fusing multiple intelligence layers alongside business rules (diversity, novelty, freshness).

Final Score = 0.4 × Supervised P() + 0.4 × RL Expected Reward + 0.1 × Diversity + 0.1 × Novelty
