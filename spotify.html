The Recommendation Engine

Machine Learning

Analyzes audio models and user behavior to create hyper-personalized playlists (like Discover Weekly). If a user plays pop, the algorithm surfaces sonically similar pop tracks to keep engagement high.

Listening Preferences

Factors in the time of day, length of listening sessions, and even the type of device used. This ensures a morning commute playlist feels drastically different from a late-night focus mix.

Collaborative Filtering

Analyzes listening habits across millions of users. If User A and User B share 80% of their tastes, the algorithm recommends the remaining 20% to each other to drive organic discovery.

User Journey

Swipe to explore
1. Home Screen
  • User can choose from 'Music or Podcast'.
  • Recommendations are based on previously streamed content.
  • Playlists based around top artists and stream mood.
  • Current playing song is visible at the bottom.
2. Exploration
  • Browse curated hubs like "Today's biggest hits" and "Your top mixes".
3. Music View
  • Selection made as per choice.
  • Shows top trends and recommendations based on past streams.
4. Podcast View
  • Recommendations based on followed content.

Strategic Enhancements

Opportunities to evolve the UX and deepen algorithmic stickiness.

Explicit Feedback Loops

Allow users to rate personalized content or explicitly mark "dislikes" to fine-tune the algorithm faster than passive listening data allows.

Algorithmic Control

Give users sliders (e.g., "More Discovery" vs "More Familiar") to actively steer their recommendation engine depending on their current mood.

Performance Tracking

North Star Metric

Monthly Active Listening Hours

The ultimate indicator of platform health. Measures the total number of hours users spend actively engaged with music, podcasts, and audio content.

L1: Playlist Engagement

Interaction

Measures deep interaction with recommended content.

  • Target Actions: Saves, Shares, Likes
  • Why it matters: Shows algorithmic relevance

L2: Discovery Conversion Rate

Exploration

Measures the conversion of passive listeners into active explorers.

  • Target Actions: Clicking to new artist profiles
  • Why it matters: Breeds long-term retention
