Netflix Product Teardown

The Product

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries and more - on thousands of internet-connected devices.

Overview

Netflix had approximately 223 million paid subscribers worldwide as of the third quarter of 2022. Most Netflix subscribers are based in the United States and Canada, accounting for over 73 million of Netflix's total global subscriber base.

Revenue Model

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming/on-demand platform making money with four plans: Mobile, Basic, Standard, Premium giving access to streamed series, movies, and shows.

Metrics Framework

North Star Metric

Retention Rate of Customers

The ultimate measure of long-term platform value, content quality, and sustainable revenue.

L1: Engagement Drivers

Watch Time Per User

Measures the depth of content consumption and active value extraction.

Completion Rate

Indicates the quality and relevance of content recommendations.

Sessions Per Day

Tracks user habituation and how frequently the app is opened.

L2: Acquisition & Growth

Monthly Subscriptions

Tracks top-of-funnel conversion and direct user acquisition growth.

Shares Per User

Measures organic user advocacy and product virality.

Improvements in User Interface

Current Feature:

Completing the sign up process is not possible in the app. So the user has to switch between the website and app to complete the process.

Improvement:

The whole process of Signing up the profile has to facilitated in mobile app only.

Current Feature:

There is no option of internet banking available for the user to make the payment. So only two modes of payment Card and UPI are available.

Improvement:

An additional option of Internet banking option to be added so that users who don't have an UPI Id can make the payment.
